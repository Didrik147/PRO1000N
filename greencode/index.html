About Us

This project aims to promote sustainable programming practices, also known as Green Software Development. The website serves as both an educational and inspirational platform, guiding developers in creating energy-efficient, environmentally responsible, and socially conscious software systems.

Sustainable software, or green software, refers to digital solutions designed, developed, and used with minimal environmental impact while maximizing positive social contributions.

Technology that supports sustainability begins with an awareness of its role within local, regional, and global contexts. It recognizes the urgent need to regenerate systems and embrace a more responsible approach to innovation and business.

This website was made as a capstone project in the course PRO1000N at University of South-Eastern Norway (USN) by three IT students.
